About Us

Who We Are

Winger Global Foundation

Winger Global foundation is an NGO in India working together to invest in the lives of impoverished children educations, women empowerment, and senior citizens (grand old father and grandmother), and empower them to create lasting change in their own lives and communities.

Education is both the means as well as the end to a better life. Winger foundation works on the discipline that is concerned with methods of teaching and learning in schools or school-like environments as opposed to various non-formal and informal means of socialization to needy kids. Winger’s foundation is helping the poor kid’s to get a better education and also set the mission to help grandfather and grandmother.

Winger’s foundation provides platforms to challenge oppressive norms and conduct programs to increase self-confidence, awareness to get jobs, and build leadership in women. Besides, they find admission for their child/children in orphanages if the mother is not able to look after them and provide medical treatment to women in need.

People live with dignity all their life, and suddenly, one day, they are left with no choice but to beg to survive. Winger foundation also works to help the elderly and making sure that they are taken care of by their old age.

Together we can help poor kid’s to get a better education and bring hope in their lives. Together, we can bring changes and make it last.
