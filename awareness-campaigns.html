Awareness Campaigns

Voter Awareness Campaign

Our NGO aims to educate and encourage people to vote and use their legal right to vote. We help marginalised sections, women, elders and new voters to vote and come out of their homes to participate in this democratic process. Various awareness measures like spreading education on voting rights, the importance of participation in the political process and acquainting people with the process have been launched by the team. Besides this, we've been engaged in voter registration drives by motivating people to register themselves as voters. Workshops, seminars, campaigns on how the election process works, the correct usage of EVMs and taking such steps to provide greater accessibility of voting to all has been one of the main aims of our NGO.

Water Awareness Campaign

Being a precious resource, educating people on the need to save water lays at the core of our value system. Our team has launched various campaign programs to help instil the sense of responsibility towards water. Besides this, our team is constantly evolving by using various engaging techniques of communication, like Nukkad Nataks, short filming and digital campaigns. Our awareness campaigns have helped in developing community engagement, which has helped people in knowing how to judiciously use water.

Cleanliness Awareness Campaign

Cleanliness campaigns has been one of the most promoted drives in recent years. Our team is engaged in launching campaigns to spread awareness on public cleanliness, personal hygiene, waste segregation, recycling education and support the "Swachh Bharat Mission". Besides awareness measures, we have been collaborating with various schools to conduct competitions, quizzes to help children and youngsters know the importance of cleanliness in their lives. Several public rallies as a part of the cleanliness drive have been organised by us to implement it at the ground level.
