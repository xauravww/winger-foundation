Nourish a Community

Nothing is more blissful than giving food to a hungry person. At Winger Global Kitchen, we ensure a proper, high-quality diet for the growth and nourishment of those in need. Our team procures raw materials under strict supervision to guarantee that no substandard ingredients enter our kitchen.

Our vision is to ensure no one in our area goes hungry. We aim to reduce world hunger by honestly devoting ourselves to the cause of humanity and ensuring every person gets a meal. We work to include as many beneficiaries as possible, and it is our privilege to serve them.

Empower Through Education

Winger Global Foundation is focused on the holistic development of children who are the future of our nation. Cherishing this philosophy, our NGO has aimed to open the Winger Global School for underprivileged children across the country, providing them with quality education free of cost.

We aim to help our kids develop to their fullest potential so that no child remains illiterate. Breaking the shackles of ignorance and poverty can only be achieved through education. Our school will provide quality teachers and proper facilities to ensure a holistic development is possible for every child.
