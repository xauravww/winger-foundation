Our Mission

With the aim of removing hunger, Winger Global Foundation aims at forming Winger Global Kitchen for providing food to poor children and people across the country. No person should sleep hungry without filling his stomach. We believe in the power of community and the importance of ensuring that basic human needs are met with dignity and respect.

Our Programs

Comprehensive initiatives designed to address hunger and education challenges across India

Winger Global Kitchen

Providing nutritious, quality meals to poor children and people across the country. Our kitchens operate under strict hygienic conditions, ensuring every meal is prepared with care and love.

Winger Global School

Providing holistic development and quality education free of cost for underprivileged children. We focus on building the future leaders of our nation through comprehensive education.

Community Outreach

Direct community engagement programs that identify and address local needs. We work closely with communities to understand their unique challenges and provide targeted support.

Emergency Relief

Rapid response programs for natural disasters and emergencies. We provide immediate food, shelter, and educational support to affected communities during crisis situations.

Our NGO, Winger Global Foundation aims at providing holistic development of children across the country. Our primary focus are the children of the nation, who will be the future of our nation one day. Hence, cherishing the objectives of our Constitution along with keeping up with philosophical view of nurturing the children of our country, our NGO has aimed at opening up Winger Global School for poor children across the country, to provide them quality education free of cost.

We aim at developing our kids to fullest, so that no child remains illiterate. Breaking the shackles of ignorance and poverty, can be achieved through the means of education only. Every child has a right to learn. He cannot be denied the right to succeed in his life and prosper. For this, our NGO aims at providing education to all children irrespective of any social barriers and help them to develop and nurture. Our school, will aim for quality teachers, with proper facilities to children so that their holistic development on a broad perspective is possible.

Not only academics, Winger Global School will also aim at extra-curricular activities which will develop leadership, artistic and creative skills in kids. Hidden talents of the kids along with application of creative mind is necessary, which will be surely provided by the school. No stones should be unturned as far as the necessary developmental stages of a student are concerned.

Nothing is more blissful than giving food to a hungry. Feeding him with all the good quality diet is necessary so that a proper growth of their bodies is ensured. Curating good quality food, the procurement of raw materials is done by our team under proper supervision so that no substandard ingredient comes in our kitchen. Moreover, variety in the meals will also be ensured so that the food is healthy enough and there is no deficiency on any vitamin or mineral. Balanced meal along with nutritious portions will be the aim of Winger Global Kitchen, and it will feed as many needy people who comes along its way without any discrimination.

Our vision is to ensure that the needy person around us gets good quality nutritious food and that no one is hungry in our area. We aim at reducing the world hunger and play our role in this moral value by honestly devoting ourselves to the cause of humanity and ensuring that every man gets a bread in his hand. We even aim at including as many beneficiaries as we can under our NGO so that many people can be helped by us and it would be a privilege for us to work for them.
