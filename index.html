What is Artificial Insemination?

In artificial insemination system, high quality male semen is stored in the laboratory after thorough testing, preserved in frozen form in liquid nitrogen. When the female animal comes into heat, the frozen semen is put in liquid state by insemination device and fertilized in the genitals of the female. This scientific technique is the best method for livestock development and breed improvement.

Why Choose This Training?

With artificial insemination, there's no need to keep bulls. Progeny testing of bulls is possible only through AI, allowing quality assessment and better livestock management.
