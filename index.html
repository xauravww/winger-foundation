About Us

Striving To Remove Hunger

With the aim of removing hunger, Winger Global Foundation aims at forming Winger Global Kitchen for providing food to poor children and people across the country. No person should sleep hungry without filling his stomach. Good quality and a balanced meal will be our aim and it will be ensured that the food is being prepared under hygienic conditions.

Quality Food

Curating good quality food, the procurement of raw materials is done by our team under proper supervision so that no substandard ingredient comes in our kitchen. Moreover, variety in the meals will also be ensured so that the food is healthy enough and there is no deficiency on any vitamin or mineral.

Vision

Our Vision

Our vision is to ensure that the needy person around us gets good quality nutritious food and that no one is hungry in our area. As far as beneficiaries are concerned, it will include everyone from poor kids, toddlers, beggars, adults; anyone who needs food for his sustenance. We aim at reducing the world hunger and play our role in this moral value by honestly devoting ourselves to the cause of humanity and ensuring that every man gets a bread in his hand.

Winger Global School

Free Quality Education for Children

Our NGO, Winger Global Foundation aims at providing holistic development of children across the country. Our primary focus are the children of the nation, who will be the future of our nation one day. Hence, cherishing the objectives of our Constitution along with keeping up with philosophical view of nurturing the children of our country, our NGO has aimed at opening up Winger Global School for poor children across the country, to provide them quality education free of cost
