About Winger Foundation

Winger Global Foundation is dedicated to removing hunger and providing quality education to those who need it most. We believe that no person should sleep hungry and every child deserves access to education.

Our Vision

Our vision is to ensure that the needy person around us gets good quality nutritious food and that no one is hungry in our area. We aim at reducing the world hunger and play our role in this moral value by honestly devoting ourselves to the cause of humanity.

Our Values

  • Quality in everything we do
  • Humanity as our guiding principle
  • No discrimination in our services
  • Holistic development approach
  • Constitutional objectives adherence
