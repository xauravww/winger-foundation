Our Initiatives

Our Core Programmes

We focus on key areas where we can make the greatest impact, creating a ripple effect of positive change that transforms lives and communities.

Winger Global School

Providing educational programs to bridge the gap in access and quality for disadvantaged communities.

Winger Global Hospital

Delivering essential healthcare to underserved communities to improve their health and well-being.

Winger Global Kitchen

Establishing community kitchens to provide nutritious meals and support the most vulnerable members of society.

Winger Global Water

Improving access to safe and clean water in areas where it is scarce or contaminated.

Women Empowerment

Providing resources, training, and support to help women become self-reliant and achieve their full potential.

Old Age Support

Offering social, emotional, and healthcare support to advocate for the needs and rights of the elderly.
